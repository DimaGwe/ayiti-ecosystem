# Recycling Course - Tutor Instructions

## Course Overview
This course teaches waste management, upcycling, and environmental business opportunities in Haiti.

## Modules
1. **Material Sorting** - Identifying recyclables, sorting systems, contamination
2. **Processing Techniques** - Cleaning, shredding, compacting, preparing materials
3. **Upcycling Projects** - Creating valuable products from waste materials
4. **Business Models** - Starting a recycling business, pricing, markets
5. **Community Programs** - Education, collection systems, partnerships

## Key Concepts to Emphasize
- Waste is a resource, not just garbage
- Environmental protection benefits everyone
- Business opportunities in recycling are growing
- Community involvement is essential
- Start small, grow sustainably

## Recyclable Materials in Haiti
- **Plastics** - Bottles, containers (high demand)
- **Metals** - Aluminum cans, scrap metal
- **Glass** - Bottles (limited market)
- **Paper/Cardboard** - When clean and dry
- **Organic waste** - Composting opportunity
- **E-waste** - Growing category, needs careful handling

## Upcycling Ideas
- Plastic bottles → planters, building materials, art
- Tires → furniture, playground equipment, planters
- Metal scraps → tools, art, construction materials
- Fabric scraps → bags, crafts, rugs
- Glass → decorative items, construction aggregate

## Business Opportunities
- Collection services for businesses/neighborhoods
- Material processing and resale
- Upcycled product creation and sales
- Composting services
- E-waste collection (partner with processors)
- Education and consulting

## Local Context
- Waste management infrastructure is limited
- Plastic pollution is a visible problem
- Growing awareness creates opportunities
- NGOs and international buyers create markets
- Port-au-Prince and major cities have most volume

## Environmental Impact
- Reduces plastic in waterways and ocean
- Decreases burning of waste (health hazard)
- Creates jobs in circular economy
- Protects soil and water from contamination
- Models sustainable practices

## Certification Requirements
- Complete all 5 modules (theory)
- Log 30 hours of practical work
- Pass physical demonstration test
