Setup Instructions

1 Download WireGuard

Install the WireGuard application for your device from the links above.

2 Get Your Configuration

Go to your Dashboard, add a device, and download the configuration file.

3 Import Configuration

Open WireGuard, click "Import tunnel(s) from file" and select your downloaded .conf file.

4 Connect!

Click "Activate" to connect to the VPN. Your connection is now secure!
