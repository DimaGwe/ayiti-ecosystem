Frequently Asked Questions

What is WireGuard?

WireGuard is a modern, fast, and secure VPN protocol. It uses state-of-the-art cryptography and is designed to be simpler and more efficient than older protocols like OpenVPN.

How do I get credits?

Credits are earned across the Ayiti Ecosystem. Complete lessons, answer trivia questions, generate solar energy, or recycle materials to earn credits that can be spent on VPN subscriptions.

Can I use VPN on multiple devices?

Yes! Depending on your plan, you can connect multiple devices simultaneously. Each device gets its own configuration file that you import into the WireGuard app.

What happens when my subscription expires?

If auto-renewal is enabled and you have enough credits, your subscription will automatically renew. Otherwise, your VPN access will be paused until you renew manually.
